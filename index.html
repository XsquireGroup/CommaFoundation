Our Programs

Four Programs.
One Mission.

Each program targets a specific population with focused, measurable impact. Built for real people in real situations.

The Comma

Emergency stabilization for working families. One-time assistance that prevents a crisis from becoming a catastrophe. Rent, utilities, car repairs — the things that break people who never stopped working.

The Ladder

Homeownership readiness for single parents. Financial literacy, down payment assistance connections, and realtor partnerships. Because owning a home isn’t a dream — it’s the next line of the story.

Beyond the Zip

Providing children access to world-class experiences, mentors, and opportunities that have nothing to do with their address. Because potential is not determined by zip code.

Still Here

Connecting incarcerated parents with their families in ways that matter. Voice recordings delivered in stuffed animals for the holidays. Live streams of their child’s games. Personal messages that arrive when it counts. A parent’s voice shouldn’t go silent because of a prison sentence.

The Founder’s Note

Why
This Exists.

When I needed a hand, I watched people count me out. Friends. Mentors. Colleagues. People I loved and trusted wrote me off without a second thought. I’ll never forget running into a colleague who had become one of my closest friends one afternoon in Cincinnati — two inches apart, eye contact, silence. He kept walking.

For 652 days I sat inside a cell and thought — this isn’t how my story goes. This isn’t how a story ends.

Very few people continued to show up when the math said walk away. I will never forget them. And I vowed to be that person for someone else who may think they’ve been forgotten.

“I built The Comma Foundation to be that moment for someone else. Not because I have all the answers. Because I know exactly what it feels like to need one person to believe the story isn’t finished. Yours isn’t either.”
Richard L. Crosby III, J.D. Founder — The Comma Foundation
